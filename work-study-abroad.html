How We Help You

Information and Guidance

Our experienced educational consultants work with each student to understand their interests, career goals, academic background, and other relevant factors to help them select the most appropriate course or program.

Admission Application

Our consultants review and provide feedback on students' personal statements, essays, recommendation letters, and other supporting documents to ensure that they meet the standards expected by the institutions.

Course Selection

At Orbital, you will find exceptional guidance on available courses that match your career choice. We are always in touch with the industry to understand job opportunities and hence give our students the best advise.

Visa Assistance

We assist students in understanding the visa regulations and requirements of the various governments abroad, and provide them with the latest information on any changes or updates that may affect their visa application.

Pre-Departure Support

Our pre-departure support services include assistance with travel arrangements, such as booking flights, arranging airport transfers, and obtaining travel visas. We also and help students understand the culture and lifestyle in countries abroad.

Accommodation Support

We provide guidance on the different accommodation options available abroad, including on-campus housing, homestays, rentals, and shared accommodations. We will also help you understand housing regulations.

Why you need us

Our Approach

Embarking on a journey to work or study abroad presents numerous challenges for Kenyans seeking international experiences. Many aspiring individuals face the fear of the unknown, unsure of where to begin or how to make their dream of working and studying abroad a reality. This lack of guidance an lead to missed opportunities and unfulfilled aspirations.

Our Solution

Enter Orbital Consultancy's Study Abroad Program - in partnership with Eldoret-based StudyLink. We specialize in providing comprehensive guidance to turn your dream into a reality. With Orbital's support, you'll no longer feel overwhelmed by the challenges of studying abroad.

Frequently asked Questions

Get all your curiosity satisfied here.

Is my money safe with your program?
Absolutely. We prioritize the security of our clients' funds. We use reputable payment gateways and ensure that all transactions are encrypted and secure.

How can I trust that your program is legitimate?
We understand your concerns. We have a proven track record of successfully assisting individuals in their work and study abroad endeavors.
What happens if there are unexpected circumstances or emergencies while I'm abroad?
We have a dedicated support team available round-the-clock to assist you in case of emergencies. Whether it's medical assistance, legal issues, or any other unforeseen circumstance, we're here to provide guidance and support.
How do you select the institutions and employers you work with?
We have a rigorous vetting process for both educational institutions and employers. We ensure that they meet our standards for quality, safety, and reliability before partnering with them.
What kind of support can I expect during my time abroad?
From the moment you enroll in our program until you return home, you'll have access to a range of support services. This includes assistance with visa applications, housing arrangements, cultural adaptation, and ongoing guidance throughout your stay abroad.
Are there any hidden costs or fees associated with your program?
Transparency is key for us. We provide detailed breakdowns of all program costs upfront, so you can rest assured that there are no hidden fees. Our goal is to make the process as transparent and hassle-free as possible for our clients.
What happens if I encounter difficulties with my host institution or employer?
Your satisfaction is our priority. If you encounter any issues during your program, we're here to advocate on your behalf and help resolve any conflicts or challenges you may face.
