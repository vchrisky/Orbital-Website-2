Our Story

Orbital Digital Marketing Agency is a dynamic and innovative tech company, registered in Kenya under the legal entity name Orbital Financial Markets Consultancy. Our journey began in 2020 with a vision to revolutionize the digital marketing landscape and empower individuals through financial education. Our flagship product, Orbital Forex Academy, stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence and growth.

Our Mission

Digital Marketing

"To propel businesses into the digital future by delivering innovative and strategic marketing solutions that elevate brands and foster lasting connections in the ever-evolving digital landscape."

Orbital Forex Academy

"To empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the complexities of financial markets, fostering financial literacy, independence, and success."

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leader in Digital Marketing and Financial Markets consultancy, adapting to meet the dynamic needs of our clients. We aim to be the catalyst for education and empowerment in financial markets.
